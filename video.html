The newest tesla car - Tesla cybertruck
Tesla Cybertruck is an upcoming all-electric battery-powered
commercial vehicle in development by Tesla, Inc. Three models have been announced,
with range estimates of 250–500 miles (400–800 km).
An estimated 0–60 mph time of 2.9–6.5 seconds, depending on the model.
Available for purchase at www.tesla.com/en_eu/cybertruck
